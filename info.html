WHY IS IT IMPORTANT FOR CONSUMERS TO SHOP FROM SUSTAINABLE COMPANIES?

Companies and manufactures are large contributors to plastic pollution in the ocean. With already 8 million pieces of plastic entering the ocean each day, itâ€™s important that we reduce our consumption by any means possible, including reducing use of products from such companies.

Coca-Cola

According to Greenpeace, Coca-Cola is the largest contributor to plastic pollution. In their packaging alone, Coca-Cola was found to have created 3 million metric tons of plastic each year. Your soda could be a huge contributor to the Great Pacific Garbage Patch - even if you don't realize it.

Karma Cola

Thankfully, there are companies that are sustainable in their plastic usage. Instead of wasteful plastic packaging for their bottles, Karma Cola uses recyclable glass bottles, making them a sustainable and eco-friendly alternative to Coca-Cola.
